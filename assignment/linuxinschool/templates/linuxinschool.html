Why should schools switch to Linux?



Everyone can see how it works!

Would you ever, as a teacher, let your whole class step into a room you and your colleagues don't know anything about? Then why would you ever let them use a close source operating system? No one knows how it works and what it does with the data you provide it, there could be a pedophile watching you from the webcam as far as the end user knows, your students personal information are important and you shouldn't just give them away to a company so easily!

Customize your system appearance!

Every school has it's own name and logo, setting a wallpaper isn't such a big customization, with Linux you can do almost whatever you want as long as you have the skill to do it! More important you can create your own distro and avoid the pain to install back all the tools the students need whenever you reinstall the system! Your students could even use their own computers and run a live instance of your distro, it will surely save you a lot of time and effort!

Give your students more work opportunities!

Most servers in the world use Linux nowadays, knowing how to use it gives your students more career opportunities, expecially if they want to work in the computer science field, this is because almost every system admin uses Linux daily, and having some basic knowledge of it surely helps, as well as simply being familiar with it!

You will avoid piracy!

Due to the fact that Linux is free, your students won't have to download an illegal version of it if they want to use it at home without buying it, as this happens with other operating systems that come with a cost, not only you are forcing them to commit a crime if they can't afford a license and they want to use the same environment they use in school, you are exposing them to malwares as they are hidden in many pirated software. Using free and open source software will keep them out of problems!
